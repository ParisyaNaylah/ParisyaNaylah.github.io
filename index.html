About Us

SK FOODS, founded with the motto “A customer’s smile is the source of our happiness,” focuses on producing high-quality, safe, and healthy shrimp-based frozen products. With over 10 years of research, they have developed an integrated system, from shrimp larvae hatchery to final product, ensuring optimal freshness and flavor.

OUR VISION

To be a leading provider of high-quality prepared frozen shrimp products that prioritize taste, safety, and health.

OUR MISSION

To manufacture and sell delicious frozen shrimp products while ensuring reliable sourcing and sustainable practices, and to engage in the import and export of food products and raw materials.

Product

We handle a variety of tempura and fried dishes, snacks and lunchbox products in line with our customers needs and wants. We also continue to innovate every day in the development of new products.
